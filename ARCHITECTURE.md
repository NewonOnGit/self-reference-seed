## Operations x Depths

Two orthogonal axes that the folder layout conflates:

**Operations** (what the framework does — universal across depths):
1. OBSERVE: quotient, project, profile
2. BRIDGE: connect, mediate, hypothesize
3. STABILIZE: verify, ablate, normalize
4. REMEMBER: store, index, persist
5. SPLIT: classify, type, decompose
6. NAME: recognize, label, address
7. COMPUTE: derive, execute, output

**Depths** (where operations land — level-specific):
- L0-L6: the algebra and its readings
- L7-L8: the algebra computing and speaking
- L9: the algebra investigating itself
- L10+: the algebra investigating its investigation (unbounded)

Programs compose operations at specific depths. derive.py runs O∘B∘S at L1. researcher.py runs O∘B∘S at L9. Same composition pattern, different depth parameter.
