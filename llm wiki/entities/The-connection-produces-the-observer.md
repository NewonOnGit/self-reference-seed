# The connection produces the observer

> **Theorem 10.3.**

nabla_s h = (1)/(2)[s, h] = (1)/(2)[ 0 & -2  \  2 & 0 ] = [ 0 & -1  \  1 & 0 ] = N

The Levi-Civita covariant derivative of the Cartan element h in the direction s is the observer N. QED [Tier A]
