# Generation strength

> **Theorem 7.1.**

The rank of ker x ker products projected onto im:

| Depth | d_K | dim(im) | rank(ker^2  im) | Fraction |
|-------|-------|------|------|----------|
| 0 | 2 | 2 | 2 | 100% |
| 1 | 4 | 8 | 8 | 100% |
| 2 | 8 | 32 | 32 | 100% |
| 3 | 16 | 128 | 64 | 50% |
| 4 | 32 | 512 | 64 | 12.5% |

The generative rank freezes at 64 while im grows exponentially. [Tier N]
