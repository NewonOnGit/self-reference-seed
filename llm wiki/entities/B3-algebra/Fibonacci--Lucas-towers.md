# Fibonacci--Lucas towers

> **Theorem 3.4.**

[R^n, N] = F(n)[R,N], \{R^n, N\} = L(n)N. Discriminant towers: disc([R^n, N]) = 20F(n)^2. Twist: (R^n N)^2 alternates I / -I. Golden convergence: 5F(n)^2 / L(n)^2  1. [Tier A]
