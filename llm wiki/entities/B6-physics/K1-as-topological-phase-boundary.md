# K1' as topological phase boundary

> **Theorem 15.3.**

*Statement.* The depth 2  3 transition exhibits the algebraic signatures of a topological phase transition.

## Dependencies

- [[Generation-strength|Generation strength]]

## Proof

*Proof.* Generation is complete (100%) at ALL depths 0-4. The depth 2→3 transition is marked by the emergence of higher Clifford structure (Cl(3,2)) and the V₄→V₁₆ transition, not by generation decay. The K1' boundary is a structural transition in the Clifford tower, not a generative freeze.
