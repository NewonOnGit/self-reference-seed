# Gauge invariance

> **Theorem 10.4.**

L(N) = sN + Ns - N = 0 and L(NR) = 0 (verified computationally). Therefore ker(L)|_{sl(2,R)}  span\{N, NR\}, which has dimension 2 = dimker(L). On a Riemannian manifold, ker(Delta_L) on symmetric 2-tensors contains the Lie derivatives L_ g (linearized diffeomorphisms). The two kernel directions correspond to the two independent infinitesimal diffeomorphisms of the 3-manifold SL(2,R) at the identity. [Tier A]
