# Neutrino mass

> **Theorem 12.4.**

m_nu = m_e * phi_bar^{34} ~ 40.1 meV, where 34 = 2(12 + 5) = 2(dim_gauge + disc).

**Exponent analysis.** The experimental window m_nu  [30, 60] meV constrains the exponent n in m_e phi_bar^n to \{33, 34, 35\} (since phi_bar^{33} * m_e = 64.8 meV, phi_bar^{34} * m_e = 40.1 meV, phi_bar^{35} * m_e = 24.8 meV). Among all expressions a * 12 + b * 5 with |a|, |b| <= 3, exactly one lands in \{33, 34, 35\}: n = 34 = 2 * 12 + 2 * 5. Neither 33 nor 35 admits a clean decomposition over \{12, 5\}.
