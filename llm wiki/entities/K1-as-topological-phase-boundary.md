# K1' as topological phase boundary

> **Theorem 15.3.**  

## Dependencies

- [[Generation-strength|Generation strength]]

## Proof sketch

*Proof.* At depth 2: $\mathrm{rank}(\ker^2 \to \mathrm{im}) = 32 = \dim(\mathrm{im})$. Generation is complete (100%). At depth 3: $\mathrm{rank}(\ker^2 \to \mathrm{im}) = 64$ while $\dim(\mathrm{im}) = 128$. Generation drops to 50%. The generative rank freezes permanently (Theorem 7.1).
