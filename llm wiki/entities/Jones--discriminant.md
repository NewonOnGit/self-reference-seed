# Jones = discriminant

> **Theorem 13.2.**

The Jones polynomial of the figure-eight knot 4_1:

V(4_1)(q) = q^{-2} - q^{-1} + 1 - q + q^2

At q = phi^2:

V(4_1)|_{q=phi^2} = (5 - 3phi) - (2 - phi) + 1 - (phi + 1) + (3phi + 2) = 5 = disc(R)

The topological invariant of the simplest hyperbolic knot at the golden quantum parameter IS the framework's discriminant. [Tier A]
