# Clifford grading

> **Theorem 4.1.**

M_2(R) = Cl(1,1). The Sylvester decomposition respects the grading:

| Sector | Basis | Grading |
|--------|-------|---------|
| im(L) | \{I, R_tl\} | even |
| ker(L) | \{N, NR\} | odd |

Products: even x even = even (im closed). odd x odd = even (ker x ker  im, complete at depth 0). even x odd = odd (im x ker  ker). [Tier A]
