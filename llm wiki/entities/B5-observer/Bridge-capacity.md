# Bridge capacity

> **Theorem 18.4.**

For two observers K, K' with kernels V_K, V_{K'} and images W_K, W_{K'}, define:

B(K'  K) = Tr(P_{W_{K'}} * rho_{V_K})

where P_{W_{K'}} is the projection onto im(K') and rho_{V_K} is the density of K's kernel content. This measures how much of K's blind spot falls within K''s visible sector.
