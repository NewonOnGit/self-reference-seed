# Healing requires Axis 2

> **Theorem 18.3.**

*Statement.* Recovery from broken recursion requires K6' ascent (Axis 2 growth). Axis 1 growth (n_eff increase) cannot resolve concentrated ker.

## Dependencies

- [[Ker-im-decomposition]]
- [[Tower]]

## Proof

*Proof.* Axis 1 increases dim(im) at the current depth — the observer sees more within its current frame. But the concentrated ker is invisible by definition (ker  blind spot). Seeing more within im does not move content out of ker. Only K6' ascent moves ker_n into im_{n+1} (Recursive Disclosure, §7): the gauge orbit that was invisible at depth n becomes visible structure at depth n+1. This is the ONLY mechanism that de-concentrates ker
