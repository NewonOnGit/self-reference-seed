# Vacuum Einstein

> **Theorem 10.5.**

The linearized vacuum Einstein equation for a metric perturbation h_{munu} on an Einstein background is Delta_L h_{munu} = 0 (modulo gauge). We have shown L_{s,s} reproduces Delta_L on sl(2,R) (Theorem 10.1), its kernel is the gauge sector (Theorem 10.4), and its non-kernel eigenvalues are +-sqrt(5) (Theorem 2.4). The stationary condition L_{s,s}(g) = 0 is therefore equivalent to the linearized vacuum Einstein equation R_{munu}^{(1)} = 0 on the SL(2,R) background.
