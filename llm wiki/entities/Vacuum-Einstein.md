# Vacuum Einstein

> **Theorem 10.5.**  The linearized vacuum Einstein equation for a metric perturbation $h_{\mu\nu}$ on an Einstein background is $\Delta_L h_{\mu\nu} = 0$ (modulo gauge). We have shown $L_{s,s}$ reproduces $\Delta_L$ on $\mathfrak{sl}(2,\mathbb{R})$ (Theorem 10.1), its kernel is the gauge sector (Theorem 10.4), and its non-kernel eigenvalues are $\pm\sqrt{5}$ (Theorem 2.4). The stationary condition $L_{s,s}(g) = 0$ is therefore equivalent to the linearized vacuum Einstein equation $R_{\mu\nu}^{(1)} = 0$ on the $\mathrm{SL}(2,\mathbb{R})$ background.
